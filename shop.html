📋 Grading Guide — Sleeve / Vinyl
MMint — perfect, unplayed
NMNear Mint — like new
VG+Very Good Plus — light marks, plays great
VGVery Good — some surface noise
G+Good Plus — plays through, noticeable wear
GGood — heavy wear, plays with noise
Format is always Sleeve / Vinyl — e.g. VG+/NM means VG+ sleeve, Near Mint vinyl.
