About KornDog Records

About Me

Music's always been in my blood — thanks to my mom and dad — but vinyl didn't hit me 'til after my mom's wreck. She didn't make it. That moment split my world in two. Records became therapy. No therapist. No pills. Just needle to groove. It grounded me. Every spin pulls me back — riding shotgun with my old man, laughing with my kids, slow dancing with my wife. This site? It's not about algorithms, trends, or fake hype. It's about soul. About stories. About memories you can hear. Every record here means something. It's not just wax — it's a vibe. A scar. A time machine. If you get it, you get it. Welcome to KornDog Records — where vinyl therapy is always on deck.

Zombie Kitty Lore

Zombie Kitty was born on Friday the 13th as a tattoo idea with my daughter. She's the spirit of every scar and every spin — stitched together from music, memories, and a little bit of chaos. When you add a record to your cart, she pops up with her blurbs — reminding you this isn't just shopping, it's resurrection. Every scratch, every groove is survival. Zombie Kitty lives in the vinyl, and she's never leaving.

Grading Standards

  • M (Mint) - Perfect condition, never played
  • NM (Near Mint) - Excellent condition, minimal handling
  • VG+ (Very Good Plus) - Light wear, great sound quality
  • VG (Very Good) - Some wear, good sound quality
  • G+ (Good Plus) - Moderate wear, acceptable sound
  • G (Good) - Considerable wear, still playable
