About KornDog Records

My Story

Music's always been in my blood — thanks to my mom and dad — but vinyl didn't hit me 'til after my mom's wreck. She didn't make it. That moment split my world in two. Records became therapy. No therapist. No pills. Just needle to groove. It grounded me.

Every spin pulls me back — riding shotgun with my old man, laughing with my kids, slow dancing with my wife. This site? It's not about algorithms, trends, or fake hype. It's about soul. About stories. About memories you can hear.

Every record here means something. It's not just wax — it's a vibe. A scar. A time machine. If you get it, you get it. Welcome to Korndog Records — where vinyl therapy is always on deck.

Meet Zombie Kitty

Zombie Kitty isn't just some random mascot — she's family. Born from a tattoo I got on a Friday the 13th with my daughter, she's a reminder that even scars tell stories. Her rose-colored eye? That's resilience. Her jagged little grin? That's survival.

Around here, she pops up with her blurbs when records hit the cart because she is the spirit of Korndog Records — a mix of pain, humor, and never dying love for music. She's not just undead; she's alive in every groove, every sale, every spin.

Zombie Kitty's Spirit:

  • Born from a Friday the 13th tattoo with family
  • Rose-colored eye symbolizes resilience through pain
  • Jagged grin represents survival and strength
  • Lives in every vinyl groove and needle drop
  • Guides customers to albums that heal the soul
  • Embodies the undead love for music that never dies
