The Origin Story

Born from a passion for music and a love for the analog warmth of vinyl, KornDog Records started as a dream in a small record store. What began as collecting rare pressings and underground gems has evolved into a curated experience for fellow music lovers.

Every record in our collection tells a story, from the classic rock anthems that shaped generations to the modern metalcore masterpieces that push boundaries. We believe music is therapy, and vinyl is the purest way to experience it.

Our Mission

At KornDog Records, we're more than just a record store. We're vinyl therapists, helping you find the perfect soundtrack to your life. Whether you're seeking the crushing riffs of metalcore or the timeless magic of classic albums, we're here to guide your journey.

Our carefully curated selection features both mainstream classics and underground treasures. From Ozzy's legendary catalog to the latest Sleep Token pressing, every record is chosen with passion and purpose.

The Future

We're constantly expanding our horizons, seeking out the next great release and building relationships with artists who share our passion for quality music. Our radio stations keep the music alive 24/7, featuring everything from the Prince of Darkness to virtual pop sensations.

The future of KornDog Records is bright, with plans for exclusive releases, artist collaborations, and even more therapeutic vinyl experiences. Stay tuned - the best is yet to come.
